What/Who/Where/When/Why

What lies beyond the farthest hill?
What lies behind the farthest wall?
What lies in the farthest depth
Of the neverending sea?

Whose voice is heard in those fragile whispers?
Whose screams are those that I hear, so faint?
Whose burrow lies far above
The ever reaching sky?

Where can I see the final star?
Where can I hold the final stone?
Where can I find the final tree
In this forever growing forest?

When will the roots be forever gone?
When will the flower take its penultimate bloom?
When will the leaf be carried away from view
By the perpetually flowing wind?

Why does the bird continue to fly?
Why do waves continue to beckon?
Why does the world continue to turn
If the sky remains unseeing?
