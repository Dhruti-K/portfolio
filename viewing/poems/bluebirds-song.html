Bluebird's Song

Lovely is the bluebird’s song
Enchanting to the ear
Carried through the winter’s air
For all who will listen

Beautiful is the melody
Filled with a kind of agony
For the bluebird has much to say
And nobody really hears

If only they could understand
The bluebird’s pleads and calls
If only someone would understand
Its chilling, longing cries

But the bird can do naught but sing
Sing, until there’s nothing left
For all that it can truly make
Is the music

The bird has seen far and wide
It has seen both the good and bad
It has seen wonders, it has seen pain
And it longs to let them know

Lonely is the bluebird’s song
Full of wishes and foolish dreams
It is so much more than simple music
If only they would know

Hopeful are the bluebird’s notes
For surely, in this world so vast
There is someone, somewhere, in a far-off land
Someone who will understand.
