Midnight Sky

The world changes
For that is its nature
It comes as no surprise

The world changes
Again and again
Until everything is wrong

Friends become foes
And trees become stumps
And fields become naught but black

But every night
There is one
That proves the world wrong

Midnight strikes
The sky is dark
It is black. It is a void.

Midnight strikes
And darkness swallows
Darkness coats me in perpetual comfort

Like an old friend
One of love and heart
The darkness is always there

Until one night
I step outside
And that, too, has left me.
