Shades of Black

How do we see
What stands before our eyes
If it is forever cloaked in darkness?

How can we discern
What is right, and what is not
If it remains this way?

How can we decide
Which path we shall walk
If we are never given light?

How are we told
That our choices are our mistakes
If there is nothing to choose?

How do the people say
That everything is clear
When all we see is dust?

And how can the world expect us
To see what we are given
To see what is before our eyes

How can the world expect us
To see its true colors
When they are all but shades of black?
